Who We Are

MADE BY FILM PEOPLE, FOR FILM PEOPLE

Dalglish Archives was born from a simple frustration: film students work incredibly hard on their final projects, and those films deserve to be preserved properly — not just uploaded and forgotten.

Dalglish Archives is a specialist Blu-ray production and packaging service dedicated entirely to film students and the academic institutions that teach them.

We understand the weight of a final film project. The late nights, the reshoots, the edit that never quite felt finished until it suddenly was. That work deserves a physical home — something you can hold, gift, and keep for the rest of your life.

Our team combines experience in post-production, graphic design, and physical media production to deliver packages that match the quality of the work inside them. From the disc mastering to the printed case, every detail is handled with care.

We work directly with students, tutors, and film departments. Whether it's a single student's passion project or an entire cohort's graduating year, we have a package to fit.

What We Do

FROM HARD DRIVE TO COLLECTOR'S PIECE

We take your finished film files and transform them into professionally authored, beautifully packaged Blu-ray products — with full support throughout.

01

RECEIVE YOUR FILM FILES

You send us your final film via our secure upload portal. We accept ProRes, H.264, H.265, and most standard export formats from editing software including Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, and Final Cut Pro.

ProResH.264H.265ResolvePremiere
02

MASTER & AUTHOR

Our team authors your film to full 1080p Blu-ray specification, including chapter marks, menus, and any additional content such as outtakes, director's commentary, and course cohort films. Every disc is tested on professional playback hardware before duplication.

1080pBD-ROMMenusChaptersOuttakes
03

DESIGN & PRINT

We design your disc face, case insert, and any additional print merchandise — posters, collector cards, gift wrap. Our designers will work from your film's existing materials (stills, title graphics, press packs) or create something from scratch.

Disc PrintCase InsertPosterCards
04

PACKAGE & DISPATCH

Individual orders are packed and dispatched directly to students. Course bulk orders are shipped to your institution. Standard turnaround is 14 working days from receipt of files; express options are available.

05

SUPPORT THROUGHOUT

A named contact manages your order from start to finish. Whether you're a solo student or a department coordinator managing 40 students, you'll have a direct line to someone who knows your project.
